DISCOVER OUR STORY

Serving Happiness Since 2018

“Makhan Maar Ke” was born out of a simple passion: to bring the authentic rich flavors of the Punjab countryside to the heart of the city. Our journey began in a small kitchen where butter was the secret ingredient to every smile.

Today, we are more than just a restaurant. We are a destination for foodies who appreciate the “buttery riot” of spices and the warmth of Indian hospitality.
